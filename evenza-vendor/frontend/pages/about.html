Our Journey

Built with passion, grown with trust

2021 — The Idea
Founded in Mumbai after witnessing the struggle photographers faced getting discovered online.
2022 — First 100 Vendors
Launched beta with 100 verified vendors in Mumbai and Delhi. 1,000 inquiries in month one.
2023 — Series A Funding
Raised ₹12 crores from top-tier investors. Expanded to 20 cities across India.
2024 — 2,800+ Vendors Nationwide
Covering 40+ cities. Launched AI recommendations and digital visiting card features.
